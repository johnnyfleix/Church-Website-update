Our Events

We have exciting events that would bring smiles and delightful memories to both you and the famiily, and also the lord himself .

Blessed Christmas

The wonderful and the greatest event of all time, the wonderful and all mighty, Jesus comes into our world .

Lenten Season

The time we Catholics dedicate our time to remebering the pains our lord and saviour, Jesus Christ bore for our sin, some of us fast during this time.

Eatser Day

The most important day in the catholic church, the day of the ressurection of Jesus, the reason this day is so special is because through his ressurection we truly believe in him.

Pentecost

This is the day that our lord descends upon his disciples and performs the miracle of speaking in tongues, this is also important because it proves that Jesus didn't have to be physically involved for miracles to work.

Crucifiction

This was the most crushing day for many; the day Jesus died, only his physical body. He remained dead for 3 days, during this time he frees souls from limbo (A place near hell).

Ascension

The most wonderful event in the Christian history; the day Jesus ascended into haeven before the eyes of his disciples, promising he would return again in Glory to judge the living and the dead.
